Meet your Masters

The Force Behind Our Training

Our team of expert lightsaber combat instructors is dedicated to bringing the thrilling world of lightsaber dueling to life. Each instructor combines extensive training with a deep passion for the art, offering a blend of technical skill, strategic insight, and creativity. Whether you're a novice eager to wield your first lightsaber or an experienced duelist looking to refine your technique, our instructors are here to guide you every step of the way. Get to know our talented team below, and see how their unique backgrounds and expertise can help you master the way of the lightsaber.

"Do or do not. There is no try."

Master Joseph Johnson

Rank: Master

Bio: Joseph Johnson is a highly experienced lightsaber combat instructor with a passion for guiding students toward mastery. When he's not teaching, Joseph is often found diving deep into ancient lightsaber lore and refining his skills in the dojo.

Specialties: Joseph specializes in Form IV - Ataru, focusing on acrobatics, speed, and fluid motion. He teaches students how to harness their agility and energy to execute dynamic, high-flying maneuvers, making them swift and unpredictable in combat.

Quote: "Master the flow, and you'll always be one step ahead."
