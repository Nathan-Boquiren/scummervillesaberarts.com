About Scummerville Saber Arts

Our Misson

At Saber Combat Academy, we believe in the power of discipline, mastery, and the ancient art of lightsaber combat. Our mission is to guide students of all skill levels on a journey that transcends the ordinary, blending the thrill of cinematic action with real-world techniques. Whether you’re just beginning to explore the ways of the saber or seeking to refine your skills to a master level, our academy is your gateway to a galaxy of adventure and personal growth. We don't just teach combat; we forge warriors, instilling the principles of focus, resilience, and respect that resonate both on and off the training floor.

A long-ish time ago, in a galaxy not so far away. . .

In the heart of the bustling city of Scummerville, a group of skilled duelists came together with one purpose: to bring the ancient ways of saber combat to life. Guided by the wisdom of both Jedi and Sith teachings, the Saber Combat Academy was born.

What began as a humble training ground has grown into a place where students from all walks of life can wield the blade, master their skills, and forge their own path in the galaxy. Now, the Academy stands as a beacon of knowledge and power, bridging the gap between myth and reality.
